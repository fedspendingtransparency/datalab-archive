Student Aid

SVG Image Here...

Federal Student Aid is financial aid provided through grants, scholarships, work-study, and loans to students for their education. This can include expenses such as tuition, housing, transportation, books, computers, and even child care.

Grants

SVG Image Here...

A federal grant is a method the government uses to fund research and implementation of ideas and projects that contribute to the betterment of the public. Grant awards can include costs associated with conducting research or studies, such as supplies, salaries, equipment, milage for travel and publication costs

Contracts

SVG Image Here...

A federal contract is a legal agreement with a government department or agency for a product or service for the benefit of the government. These contracts are governed by a strict set of terms and conditions, including clauses from the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR).
