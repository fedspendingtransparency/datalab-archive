Data Sources and Methodology

This analysis was conducted using the Monthly Treasury Statement (MTS) as the data source for federal government income of the United States. The Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) Consumer Price Index (CPI) for All Urban Consumers was used to adjust income in fiscal years 2013-2016 for inflation. Adjusting for inflation allows users to see real, not nominal, changes in sources of income over time. Gross Domestic Product (GDP) figures come from the International Monetary Fund (IMF) World Economic Outlook Database (WEOD).
