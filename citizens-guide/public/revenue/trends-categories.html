The visualization was created using Monthly Treasury Statement (MTS) as the data source for federal government revenue of the United States. The Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers (CPI-U) was used to adjust revenue in fiscal years 2014-2017 for inflation. Adjusting for inflation allows users to see real, not nominal, changes in sources of revenue over time.
