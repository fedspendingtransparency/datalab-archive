Homelessness Analysis

Explore Federal programs that address homelessness

On a single night in 2016, more than 500,000 people experienced homelessness in the United States. With our visualization uncover how individuals experiencing homlessness are counted, which federal programs are addressing homelessness, and the amount of federal spending on homelessness by region. Through this analysis you will get a glimpse of how federal funding impacts homelessness and can serve as a useful too for those working to reduce homelessness.
