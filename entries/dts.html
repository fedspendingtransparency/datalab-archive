The Daily Treasury Statement (DTS) is published each working day of the Federal Government. It provides data on the cash and debt operations of the Treasury based upon reporting of the Treasury account balances by Federal Reserve banks. For more information about the dataset used on this story, please go to: https://www.fms.treas.gov/dts/index.html
