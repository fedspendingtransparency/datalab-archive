Federal Debt Trends over Time

Debt Debt to GDP

One way to gauge the sustainability of federal finances is to observe how the federal debt has changed over time. The U.S. has experienced a deficit in each year since 2001, with a spike from 2009 to 2012 because of the financial crisis and ensuing recession.

What is sustainable policy?

According to the Financial Report of the United States Government (FR) government spending is considered to be sustainable when deficit spending as a percentage of gross domestic product is steady or declining.
